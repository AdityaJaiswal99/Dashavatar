Dashavatar of Lord Krishna

1. Matsya Avatar

Matsya is the half fish, a half-human form of Krishna. According to Matsya Puran, Matsya informs Manu, the leader of the humans, of the great flood and helps him save all the motile living beings, the Vedas, and the seeds of all plants.

2. Kurma Avatar

Kurma is the half tortoise half-man form of Krishna. During the churning of the ocean (Samudra Manthan), he balances Mount Mandara on his shell to assist the gods and the demons in the churning process. He also have supported the weight of the cosmos on his back.

3. Varaha Avatar

Varaha is the half man and half boar avatar of Krishna. In Hindu dharma, he slays the demon Hiranyaksha to save Bhudevi, the personification of the earth and restores her back to the surface from a sinking state using his tusks.

4. Narsingha Avatar

Narsimha is the half lion and a half-human avatar of Krishna. He was born to end the reign of the demonic king Hiranya Kashyap and establish peace, order, righteousness, and other elements of dharma on earth.

(read more)

5. Vamana Avatar

The 5th avatar of Krishna. Vamana, was a dwarf Brahmin. This avatar of Lord Vishnu comes to check the growing power of the demonic king Mahabali, which he does by tricking him during a sacrificing ceremony and sending him to the underworld.

7. Rama Avatar

Lord Rama is one of the most significant and powerful deities of Hindu dharma and the lead character of the epic Ramayana. He kills the evil king Ravana to end his terrorizing rule and free his wife Sita, whom Ravana had kidnapped.

8. Balarama Avatar

The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna, is the fountainhead of all incarnations. He assists in Lord Krishna's transcendental pastimes. He is the source of the entire spiritual world and is the adi-guru, the original spiritual master.He Himself helps in the pastimes of Lord Krishna. In the form of Lord Sesa He serves Sri Krishna in various ways. In all the forms He tastes the transcendental bliss of serving Krishna. No one can approach Krishna without first getting the mercy of Baladeva.

9. Buddha Avatar

Siddhartha Gautam, later known as Gautam Buddha, left his family and all material possession in search of enlightenment. He founded Buddhism and taught people ways to end all kinds of suffering through the Noble Eightfold Paths.

10. Kalki Avatar

Kalki is the only avatar of Krishna that is yet to be born. He will end all evil by defeating the demon Kali and start a new Satyayuga. Kali is all the negative emotions and elements personified into one. Kalki is depicted as a warrior riding a white horse and holding a shining sword.
